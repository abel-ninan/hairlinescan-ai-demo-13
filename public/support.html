Frequently Asked Questions

How does HairlineScan work?

HairlineScan uses AI to analyze photos of your hairline and provide educational information about hair health patterns. Simply take photos and receive instant insights.

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. HairlineScan is for educational purposes only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a dermatologist for medical concerns.

Are my photos stored?

Your photos are processed temporarily for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why did my analysis fail?

This can happen due to poor lighting, blurry photos, or temporary server issues. Try retaking photos in good lighting and ensure your hairline is clearly visible.

How do I get the best results?

Use good lighting (natural light works best), hold the camera steady, and take photos from multiple angles (front, left side, right side).
