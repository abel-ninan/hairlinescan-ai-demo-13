Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

HairlineScan ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: HairlineScan is an entertainment app only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All results are for fun and are not scientifically validated.

Information We Collect

Photos

  • We request access to your device camera to capture photos of your hairline for analysis.
  • Photos are processed temporarily for AI analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Photos are transmitted securely via HTTPS to our analysis service.

Questionnaire Responses

  • We collect optional questionnaire responses (age range, family history, etc.) to provide more relevant entertainment information.
  • This information is processed with your photos and is not stored permanently.

Device Information

  • We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered entertainment analysis of hairline photos
  • To generate personalized entertainment tips based on your responses
  • To improve our app and services

We do not use your information for advertising, sell your data to third parties, or create user profiles.

Data Storage and Security

  • Photos and questionnaire data are processed in real-time and not permanently stored.
  • All data transmission uses industry-standard HTTPS encryption.
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase, Google Cloud) for processing.
  • Analysis results are displayed locally on your device and not stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For image analysis (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase: For secure serverless functions (subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny camera access (the app will not function without it)
  • Request information about data processing
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

HairlineScan is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
